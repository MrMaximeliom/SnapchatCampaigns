Here are the steps you need to take to get you started:

1.Register as Snapchat Developer:
Go to the Snapchat Developer Portal (https://developers.snapchat.com)
Sign up for a developer account if you don't have one already.
Complete the required registration steps and agree to the terms and conditions.
2.Create a Snapchat Business Account:
If you don't already have a Snapchat Business Account, create one by visiting the Snapchat Ads Manager (https://ads.snapchat.com)
Follow the instructions to set up your business account, including providing your business details and payment information.
3.Create a Snapchat App:
In the Snapchat Developer Portal, navigate to the "My Apps" section.
Click on "Create App" and provide the necessary details, such as the app name, description, and logo.
Select the "Snapchat Ads" product and choose the appropriate app permissions based on your requirements.
4.Generate API Credentials:
After creating the app, go to the "My Apps" section and click on your app's name.
Under the "Keys" tab, click on "Create" to generate the necessary API credentials.
Take note of the generated Client ID and Client Secret as you'll need them in the next steps.
5.Set Up Authentication::
Choose the appropriate authentication method based on your app's requirements. Snapchat supports OAuth 2.0 for authentication.
Implement the authentication flow in your app to obtain an access token.

After completing the above steps, we need the following keys to help you create your audience segments:

  • Client ID
  • Client Secret
  • Redirect URI
  • Account ID
